The Vaccinator

This App has been realized as part of our first group project at UC Berkeley coding Bootcamp
It allows the user to look for a specific state inside the US and get COVID-19 related data, vaccination data, and where to find available vaccine appointments.
The App also includes a color-coded and interactive map with real-time data to have an idea of the Covid-19 vaccination progress in the US.
